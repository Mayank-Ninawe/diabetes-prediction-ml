Dataset: Pima Indians Diabetes Database

Source: Kaggle
URL: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/uciml/pima-indians-diabetes-database

Description:
- 768 samples
- 8 features (Pregnancies, Glucose, BloodPressure, SkinThickness, Insulin, BMI, DiabetesPedigreeFunction, Age)
- 1 target (Outcome: 0=No Diabetes, 1=Diabetes)
- Class distribution: 500 non-diabetic (65%), 268 diabetic (35%)

Citation:

Using the ADAP learning algorithm to forecast the onset of diabetes mellitus. 
In Proceedings of the Symposium on Computer Applications and Medical Care (pp. 261--265). 
IEEE Computer Society Press.
